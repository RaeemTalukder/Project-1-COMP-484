Tank

Tank is the class archetype design to block damage and take space. Tanks usually are in the front line of the team "tanking" the damage for the rest of the team. They usually have high health and defense, but low damage output. The tanks main goal is to hold position or space denying the opponent advangateous position. Without a Tank not only does damage have less space to work with almost always getting forced back due to their low health pools. Tanks should always stick to the two core funetmentals which is protecting the teama and taking space.
