Support

Support is the class archetype that aims to either heal or enchance the other memebers of the team. The main goal of a support is to keep the team alive and to empower them. They usually have low health and damage but compensate witht the ability to heal and empower which both damage and tank lack Without Support Tanks and Damage would always be widdled down to death without proper support keeping them afloat. The core two funtementals of a support is survival and empowerment.
